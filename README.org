* 15 Puzzle

Basic =15 Puzzle=. Click to select, click the hole again to move.

There's also =S= for =Scramble=-ing (implemented as a random one, iterated
trials until we get one with proper parity).

This was brought about because I saw from Mathologer than you can
check the validity of a random setting by only checking parity. Isn't
it neat that that's possible?
